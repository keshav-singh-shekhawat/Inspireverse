Steve Jobs

Innovation, risk and reinvention — a complex genius who reshaped technology.

Steve Jobs co-founded Apple Inc., transforming technology and design worldwide. Born in 1955 in San Francisco, he was adopted and displayed a passion for electronics from a young age.

He was ousted from Apple in 1985 but returned to revolutionize the company with products like the iPhone, iPad, and MacBook. Jobs' story is a testament to vision, risk-taking, and relentless pursuit of excellence.

His work continues to influence design, technology, and entrepreneurship around the world.
