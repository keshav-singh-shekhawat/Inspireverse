Elon Musk

Ambition, endurance, and big bets — building toward the future.

Elon Musk, born in South Africa in 1971, is an entrepreneur and innovator. He co-founded companies like Zip2, X.com (PayPal), Tesla, SpaceX, and Neuralink. Musk's work focuses on sustainable energy, space exploration, and cutting-edge technology.

His story is one of extreme ambition, perseverance through failures, and constant reinvention. Musk envisions a future where humanity is multiplanetary and sustainable.
