Shah Rukh Khan

From television actor to King of Bollywood — a story of grit, charm, and persistence.

Shah Rukh Khan, often called the "King of Bollywood", started his career in television before making a breakthrough in films. Born in New Delhi in 1965, SRK faced hardships early on but pursued his passion for acting relentlessly.

He debuted in Bollywood with the film "Deewana" (1992) and quickly became one of the most loved actors. SRK's career spans over 30 years, featuring iconic movies like "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge", "My Name is Khan", and "Chak De! India".

Beyond films, Shah Rukh Khan is a successful entrepreneur and philanthropist, supporting causes in child health and education. His story is one of determination, talent, and sheer hard work.
